LE Business Hierarchy

Each occurrence of LE Business Hierarchy identifies, names, and defines one taxonomy or organizational framework that has meaning to one Business Legal Entity. Each Business Legal Entity may establish any number of LE Business Hierarchies but each such hierarchy is defined only for that one Legal Entity. The establishment of LE Business Hierarchies is optional and a Business Legal Entity is not required to define any such structures. Each LE Business Hierarchy is in turn made up of one or more categories, sub strata, or lower-level grouping defined by occurrences of LE Business Hierarchy Category entity. There is no restriction on the type of information categorized by a Business Hierarchy as indicated by the Business Hierarchy Type attribute. For example, the hierarchy may define a specific geographic breakdown used by the Legal Entity or may provide a product breakdown by product line, etc. .
