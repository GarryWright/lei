LE Corporate Event

Each occurrence of LE Corporate Event defines one Business Legal Entity effected by one Corporate Event. Each Corporate Event may impact one or multiple Business Legal Entities (e.g., merger, acquistion, etc.). If the Corporate Event impacts "n" number of LE's, then there will be "n" occurrence of LE Corporate Event, one for each Business LE impacted by the event.

Attributes

The attributes show relationships between the data group and none-group data nodes, these are data nodes that carry individual pieces of information
