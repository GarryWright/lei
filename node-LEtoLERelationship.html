LE to LE Relationship

Each occurrence of LE to LE Relationship defines a pair of two Legal Entities that are related to each other.

Attributes

The attributes show relationships between the data group and none-group data nodes, these are data nodes that carry individual pieces of information

Name Type Documentation Sample Value Available Sources
RelationshipRole Enumeration A code that explains how two LE's are related to each other. ULTIMATE PARENT
OwnershipPercentage Number The percent of ownership of a Secondary Legal Entity held by the Parent or Primary Legal Entity 0.6525 AVOX
RelationshipStartDate Date The earliest date that a Legal Entity Relationship was in effect. 01-Jan-1955
RelationshipEndDate Date The latest date that a Legal Entity Relationship was in effect. "--"
Date/TimeLastReviewed DateTime The date and time this LE data was formally reviewed. 15-Dec-2012
