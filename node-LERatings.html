LE Ratings

Each occurrence of LE Ratings contains one time-sensitive score or evaluation of the credit worthiness of a Legal Entity that has been published by a Data Source, usually a credit rating agency. Each LE Rating represents that Data Source's evaluation of qualitative and quantitative information about the LE including both public and in some cases, private information obtained by the rating agency.
