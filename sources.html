Sources

The Atlas model allows for the definition of source references on attributes, this allows you to see how attributes from other models can be incorporated into the model. The concept of multi-source modeling ensures that we are able to pull information from multiple places together and more importantly keep the meta-data around that sourcing in one place.

Below is a breakdown of each of the available sources in the model

MARKIT

The following attributes are available from this source

Source Attribute Target Attribute Description
MARKITENTITYTYPE Legal Entity Sub Type The sub-type of entity determines reporting requirements and hierarchy methodologies to be used
MARKITENTITYLEGALFORM Legal Form The legal structure for the LE. All developed countries have their own version of corporate law that defines the types of corporations that may be formed in their country anfd how they will be registered and regulated.
MARKITTICKER LE Identifier Value The character string that identifies the Legal Entity as designated by the Identifier Type.
MARKITENTITYNAME Official Business Name The Official Business Name, if different from the Legal Entity Name.
MARKETIDENTIFIERCODE LE Identifier Value The character string that identifies the Legal Entity as designated by the Identifier Type.
