Corporate Event

Each occurrence of Corporate Event identifies and provides summary information regarding one action, event, or activity occuring in the past or scheduled to occur in the future that has significant effect on one Business Legal Entity (e.g., Shareholder Meeting) or on multiple Business Legal Entities (e.g., merger or acquistion) as provided by one Data Source. The full terms, conditions, and detail informaton regarding the Corporate Event itself are not stored in Legal Entity Data Domain but are instead stored separately outside the domain space and are referenced by the Corporate Event ID attribute. The specific one or multiple Business LEs impacted by the Corporate Event are identified by separate occurrences of LE Corporate Event entity.

Attributes

The attributes show relationships between the data group and none-group data nodes, these are data nodes that carry individual pieces of information
